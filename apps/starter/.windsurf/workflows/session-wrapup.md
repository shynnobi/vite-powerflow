2. List options considered and why they were accepted/rejected.
3. State the final decision and expected consequences.
4. Capture key constraints and assumptions used.
5. Identify follow-up actions and open technical questions.
6. Mention documentation updates needed (if any).
7. Provide a concise handoff summary for PR/next session reuse.
